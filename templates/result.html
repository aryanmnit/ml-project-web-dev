Thinker: The Philosopher

The Thinker personality type is analytical, logical, and deeply curious,
always seeking to understand complex systems and concepts. They excel at problem-solving,
enjoy intellectual challenges, and prefer objective reasoning over emotional decision-making.

{% elif result == "Introvert" %}

Introvert: The Researcher

The Introvert personality type is reflective, thoughtful,
and energized by solitude, preferring deep conversations over small talk.They excel in independent work,
focusing on creativity, analysis, or problem-solving in quiet environments.

{% elif result == "Extrovert" %}

Extrovert: The Entrepreneur

The Extrovert personality type is outgoing, energetic, and thrives
in social settings, gaining energy from interactions with others. They excel in dynamic
environments, enjoy teamwork, and are often expressive, enthusiastic, and confident communicators.

{% elif result == "Sensitive" %}

Sensitive: The Artist

The Sensitive personality type is deeply empathetic, intuitive, and emotionally aware, often attuned
to the feelings of others and their surroundings. They are compassionate, creative,
and introspective, thriving in environments that allow them to express emotions and help others.

{% elif result == "Planner" %}

Planner: The Architect

The Planner personality type is highly organized, detail-oriented, and thrives
on structure, strategy, and long-term vision. They excel at setting goals,
managing resources efficiently, and ensuring smooth execution of tasks.

{% else %}

Explorer: The Archaeologist

The Explorer personality type is adventurous, curious, and thrives
on new experiences, constantly seeking to discover the unknown. They are independent,
open-minded, and fearless in facing challenges, whether in travel, innovation, or problem-solving.
